About Dinmukhamed Konayev

Dinmukhamed Konayev (1912-1993) was a Soviet state and public figure, three-time Hero of Socialist Labor, academician of the Kazakh SSR Academy of Sciences, and Doctor of Technical Sciences.

Important Events

  1. 1912 - Born in Almaty region.
  2. 1936 - Graduated from the Moscow Institute of Nonferrous Metals.
  3. 1960 - Elected as the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Kazakhstan.
  4. 1964-1986 - Period of leadership in Kazakhstan.
  5. 1993 - Died in Almaty.

Contributions

  • Accelerated the industrial development of Kazakhstan by opening new factories and enterprises.
  • Contributed to the growth of Almaty as a cultural and scientific center.
